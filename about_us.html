Pastor John Daniel's Message

Welcome to FGAG Church! It is our joy to serve the Lord together and grow in His grace. May you find your spiritual home with us, and may His love abound in your life.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to glorify God by making disciples of Christ through love, service, and worship. We envision a community deeply rooted in God’s Word, reflecting His light in every corner of society.

Our Church History

Started as a humble prayer gathering, FGAG Church has grown into a vibrant spiritual family, nurturing lives and serving the city for decades. We thank God for His continuous faithfulness.
