Friends Garden AG Church

Friends Garden A.G Church is part of Assemblies of God India. We began in 2019, and our church is located in Kollidam, Mayiladuthurai District. Today, we are blessed to have more than 25 families as part of our church community.

Right after we started, we faced a big challenge — the COVID-19 pandemic. The church was closed for almost one and a half years, but even during that time, people stayed faithful to the fellowship. By God’s grace, we continued to grow, little by little.

Pastor Amal M. Augustine's Message

Welcome to FGAG Church! It is our joy to serve the Lord together and grow in His grace. May you find your spiritual home with us, and may His love abound in your life.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Church History

Started as a humble prayer gathering, FGAG Church has grown into a vibrant spiritual family, nurturing lives and serving the city for decades. We thank God for His continuous faithfulness.
